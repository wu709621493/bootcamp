* The tube links the Mekong delta and Hong Kong harbor, symbolizing a channel for stories, trade, and everyday conversation across regions.
* Waves and arrows point toward Hong Kong, depicting messages traveling along shared waters toward bustling communication hubs.
* The double equals sign `===` hints at fiber lines and ferries alike, blending digital and physical routes of connection.
